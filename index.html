My name is David. For over 30 years I've worked in IT — a journey that began in the 1990s, operating mainframe computers like the IBM System 38 and AS/400 for banks in central London, and evolved through IT support into consultancy for VIP clients.

Throughout that time, I've always had a creative life running alongside the technical one. Music has been a constant — producing original tracks using hardware synthesisers, drum machines and keys, with two of my productions available on iTunes and a back catalogue on SoundCloud.

I see AI the same way I saw the internet arriving in the mid-90s: a shift that opens doors. It's a tool, not a replacement for human creativity. Today I'm moving deliberately towards web development — combining decades of technical precision with the creative instinct I've never stopped developing.
