David is an IT professional with over 30 years of hands-on experience — no jargon, no runaround, just someone who gets things sorted. He's spent his career working directly with CEOs and senior figures at companies including BMI Group, Creed, and Novacroft, and understands that people at that level need things done properly, quickly, and with minimal fuss.

With more than 20 years on the Mac platform, David has a deep knowledge of Apple ecosystems alongside Windows environments. He bridges the gap between complex IT and the people who just need it to work — and he always goes further than the bare minimum.

Outside of client work, David produces music independently using Logic Pro, has two releases on iTunes, and runs Autofacts-Bespoke — an automotive YouTube channel. His interests span music production, brand design, and web design with a difference. He's not your typical IT bloke. But he is exactly that, too.
